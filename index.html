Data Deletion

How to request deletion of your data

Genius OCR does not store sensitive personal data beyond what is needed to create and manage your account (name, email, provider, provider_id). If you would like to request deletion of your account or any stored data, please follow the steps below.

Request deletion by email

  1. Send an email to magicwithshan@gmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. In the email body, include the email address associated with your Genius OCR account and any other identifying details.
  3. We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours and will process deletion within 7 days.

What we will delete

We will remove your account record and any associated profile information stored in our database (email, name, provider, provider_id). Any content you created/uploaded (if applicable) will also be removed upon request.

Contact

If you have questions about deletion, contact us at magicwithshan@gmail.com.
